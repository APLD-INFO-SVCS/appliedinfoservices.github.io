Applied Information Labs is the R&D and tooling division of Applied Information Services, LLC. It produces internal instruments, analytical tools, and experimental systems in support of OSINT operations, ISR planning, and collection architecture design. Access is restricted to authorized personnel.

VIGIL
ISR Flight Pattern Planner
ACTIVE

Generate georeferenced ISR orbit waypoints across eight flight patterns — circular, racetrack, figure-8, expanding square, parallel track, sector search, and AR track — with profile compliance checking and export to SkyVector, SimBrief, and plain-text formats.

APV
Applied Flight Visualizer
ACTIVE

Interactive flight-route visualization with great-circle arc rendering over a MapLibre basemap. Accepts IATA codes, ICAO codes, or raw coordinates across a database of ~4,400 civilian airports and ~570 military installations worldwide.

Launch APV
AFC
AIS Fee Calculator
ACTIVE

Two-module financial instrument implementing the AFM fee model. The Time Value Tool derives a fully-loaded hourly floor rate and service-tier benchmarks from compensation inputs. The Job Quote Tool builds itemized quotes with subcontractor labor, overhead allocation, rush pricing, and a three-phase invoice schedule.

Launch AFC
TRIM
KML Waypoint Reducer
ACTIVE

Simplifies a recorded KML/GPS track down to a clean waypoint set via Douglas-Peucker line simplification, with a live before/after graph and export to SkyVector, SimBrief, and plain-text formats. Runs entirely client-side — nothing is uploaded.
